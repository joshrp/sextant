# Manifolds

Manifolds are a key concept in the calculator that help manage resource flows in your factory.

## What is a Manifold?

A manifold represents a group of product flows that are connected together. When you have multiple production chains sharing the same resource, the calculator groups these connections into a manifold.

## How Manifolds Work

- Manifolds appear in the sidebar when your factory has connected production chains
- Each manifold shows the product type and the total flow rate
- You can free a manifold to allow the solver to optimize it independently

## Using Manifolds

Click on a manifold in the sidebar to:
- View detailed information about the product flows
- Toggle whether the manifold is constrained or free
- Highlight the manifold's connections in the graph

## Free vs Constrained Manifolds

- **Constrained**: The solver must balance inputs and outputs exactly
- **Free**: The solver can adjust flows independently for optimal results
