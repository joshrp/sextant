# Introduction

Welcome to the **COI Calculator** - a production planning tool for Captain of Industry!

## What is this?

The COI Calculator helps you plan and optimize your factory layouts in Captain of Industry. It uses linear programming to find the most efficient resource flows and production ratios.

## Key Features

- **Visual Factory Builder**: Drag and drop recipes to build your production chains
- **Automatic Optimization**: The solver finds optimal production ratios automatically
- **Resource Tracking**: See exactly what goes in and out of your factory
- **Multiple Zones**: Organize different production areas
- **Import/Export**: Save and share your factory designs

## Getting Started

1. **Create a Zone**: Start by creating a production zone for your factory
2. **Add Recipes**: Add recipe nodes for the production buildings you want
3. **Connect Products**: Draw connections between recipes to route products
4. **Set Goals**: Define what you want to produce or consume
5. **Solve**: Let the calculator optimize your production ratios
