# Import & Export

Importing and exporting can be done from the settings menu. You can export individual factories or do a bulk export of everything or a selection across all zones.

You'll get an encoded string that represents the entire factory — its recipes, layout, connections, and goals.

Copy that string and save it somewhere safe, or paste it into a message to share it.

Importing is also in settings and will allow you to rename factories and zones, and to move factories between zones before the actual import. 

## Versioning

Export strings are versioned internally. Older exports can always be imported into newer versions of Sextant. I will do my best to maintain this compatibility, but I recommend keeping backups of your exports just in case.
