# Thermal Storage

Thermal storage buildings store steam as heat and retrieve it later. In-game, this uses an intermediate heat product — but here the heat is abstracted away and the building is modeled as a steam-and-water conversion with configurable loss.

Steam inputs are converted to water at the loss rate while water is converted to steam at the same loss rate. 

## Idle losses

In game, an idle thermal storage looses heat over time, this is hard to model since factories can be burst use or continuous use.

To control this, change the slider according to how much you expect this building to be used. 

- 0% means no loss, the building is never idle and in continuous use. 

- 100% means all steam is lost, the building is always idle and never used.

## Throughput

Each thermal storage building handles up to **1,800 steam per 60 seconds** in-game.
