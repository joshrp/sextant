# Balancers

Balancers are special nodes that manage resource flows in and out of your factory.

## What is a Balancer?

In Captain of Industry, balancers (also called Import/Export buildings) allow you to:
- Import resources from outside the factory
- Export products to other parts of your island
- Balance resource flows between production chains

## Using Balancers in the Calculator

When you drag a product connection to empty space and select a balancer, it creates a special node that:
- Has unlimited capacity for the selected product
- Can act as either a source (import) or sink (export)
- Helps balance resource flows in your optimization

## When to Use Balancers

- **Imports**: When you need raw materials from outside the factory
- **Exports**: When you want to send products elsewhere
- **Buffering**: To smooth out production imbalances
- **Testing**: To quickly add sources/sinks while designing

## Balancer Options

Each balancer node can be configured to:
- Handle a specific product type
- Import or export (direction can be flipped like any recipe node)
- Integrate with your goals and constraints
