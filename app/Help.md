# Help


## Recycling

Due to the way Recycling works in Captain of Industry there are some unintuitive aspects to calculating the scrap producted by waste sorters processing Recycling materials.

The Wiki Page here explains some of the unintuitive aspects in game https://wiki.coigame.com/Recyclables, namely that different items produce scrap at different rates. To model this in the calculator the waste sorter walks the tree, gathers up the items that produce recycling and calculate it.

**Warning** this means if you have recycling output not modeled by the graph here, but you DO recycle it in game, your scrap outputs will differ. Even if you use different sorters. COI shares all recycling globally between all sorters, regardless of routing.
